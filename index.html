Shree Sitala Computer Lab

Establish full-fledged computer lab in Pawai Gaude, Syangja. Provide the opportunities for the communities to have access to internet where they can learn and implement effective ways of agricultural procedures, communicate using email, and consume internet-based content provided by the government.

Learn More Donate Now!

Objectives

Essential Skills

To provide essential computer, softwares, and internet skills to the students from an early age 10.


Basic Skills

To provide and train the basic ideas of Email service, Microsoft Office Word, and typing for teachers, community members, and local government officials.

Advanced Skills

Eventually to build-up higher level skills such as mapping, and data collection making the work useful for local governance.

Programs

Computer Lab

A sophisticated computer lab will be established inside the school premises with 15-20 computers, all with the internet facilities.




Computer Courses

Different levels of computer courses will be provided starting from the class 6 to the most senior students (Bachelor).




Instructors

An instructor selected by the board committee will train the senior (9-12) students in the first year. Some students from the senior level will be selected (as a volunteer) to train and support the students from grade 6 - grade 9.

Internet

With the help of Nepal Telecom and local government, an internet service will be started in the school and area.




Lab Management

A room will be made available by the Sheetala HSS. The computer teacher shall be the incharge of the computer lab.




Support Local Government

The computer facility shall also be used to conduct trainings to support the local government.
